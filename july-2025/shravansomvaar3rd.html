About The Puja

Laghu Rudri is a very powerful hymn from the Yajur Veda dedicated to Lord Rudra (Shiva). It is chanted to seek his blessings for removing obstacles, success, growth and protection. This Puja is believed to be one of the most powerful Puja devoted to Lord Shiva.

Why Is This Puja For You?

  • For the 3rd Shravan Somvaar, we have organized the Laghu Rudra Abhishek through 11 Vedic pandits with a variety of offerings that hold important spiritual meanings, each contributing uniquely to the ritual's significance.
  • In this Puja, we will perform Abhishek using water from seven rivers, sugarcane juice, pomegranate juice, rose water, milk, mustard oil, and curd, seeking blessings for purifying the mind and body, invoking divine blessings, promoting spiritual growth, and seeking prosperity and well-being.
  • This Puja will be held on the 28th July on the auspicious occasion of 3rd Shravan Somvar at 2:50 PM (IST) at Vijay Muhurat through 11 Vedic Pandits, spanning 3 to 4 hours.
  • Your Sankalp will be taken as per your full name and Gotra as guided in our Vedic scriptures. You will also receive a small video separately through WhatsApp in which our Vedic Pandit will be chanting your name and Gotra.

Planet : Chandra & Ketu

About The Puja

Shiva represents the transcendent and unchanging aspect, while Shakti represents the dynamic and creative energy. Together, they form a divine partnership that is central to the cosmic order and the process of creation. It’s a reminder that every one of us carries both strength and tenderness, silence and song, stillness and movement.

Why Is This Puja For You?

  • This Vedic Puja seeks blessings of Shiva-Shakti for bringing inner balance and harmony, igniting our inner energy for vitality, and guiding us towards deeper relationships and spiritual growth.
  • Shiv Shakti Maha Puja will be conducted on 3rd November at 12:40 PM (IST) as per Abhijeet Muhurat by 6 Vedic Brahmins and this puja will be live streamed.
  • It involves rituals like Rudri Path, Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra Jaap 11000 times, Navarna Mantra with 9 Malas for the blessings of Lord Shiva and Goddess Shakti.
  • Your Sankalp will be taken as per your full name and Gotra as guided in our Vedic scriptures. You will also receive a small video separately through WhatsApp in which our Vedic Pandit will be chanting your name and Gotra.

Steps of the Puja

Shanti Paath, Devata Namaskar, Maha Sankalp, Ganga Pujan, Hanuman Pujan, Bhairav Pujan, Kuldevi Pujan, Pitru Namaskar, Guru Vandana, Ganapati Pujan, Maha Abhishek (Ganapati, Shiv, Parvati, Durga), Shodasmatrika Sthapan, Pradhan Sthapan, Navgrah Sthapan, Rudri Paath and Shri Sukta and Durga Kawach, Shodasopchar Pujan, Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra Japa (11000 times), Navarna Mantra Japa with 9 Mala, Havan, Dashansh Trapan Marjan, Brahmin Bhojan, Aarti, Pushpanjali, Kshama Prarthana, Maha Prasad, Ashirvad.

Puja Prasad For You

Shiv Shakti Yantra to be kept in Puja place, Dry sweets safe to consume which are offered to God, Mauli to be tied around the wrists of family members, Akshat, Chandan which shall be applied on the forehead.

Kaal Bhairav Aradhana

12 Nov 2025 (Wednesday)

11:50am (Shubh Muhurat) IST

7 Pandits

Planet : Chandra

About The Puja

Kal Bhairav is a fierce and formidable manifestation of Lord Shiva, often depicted as a fierce deity with a black complexion and carrying a trident. He is considered the guardian of the city of Kashi (Varanasi). Kal Bhairav is known for his role as the protector of the holy city and is believed to be the enforcer of dharma (righteousness) and justice.

Why Is This Puja For You?

  • Devotees of Kal Bhairav seek his blessings for protection from negative influences, removal of obstacles, and to dispel fear and insecurity. He is associated with the concept of time and the inevitability of death, emphasizing the transient nature of life.
  • This Puja is performed for seeking protection against negative energies, overcoming ailments, longevity of life, to rid oneself from fears, success in court cases and for good fortune.
  • This Puja will be live streamed on the 12nd November at 11:50 PM as per Shubh Muhurat by 7 Vedic Brahmins.
  • It involves Vedic rituals like Bhairav Mantra japa, Kaal Bhairav Ashtak Stotra paath, Kaal Bhairav Havan and Maha Lakshmi Havan for the blessing of Lord Kaal Bhairav.
  • Your Sankalp will be taken as per your full name and Gotra as guided in our Vedic scriptures. You will also receive a small video separately through WhatsApp in which our Vedic Pandit will be chanting your name and Gotra.

Steps of the Puja

Shanti Paath, Devata Namaskar, Maha Sankalp, Ganga Pujan, Prithvi Pujan, Deepak Pujan, Surya Pujan, Dasho Disha Puajn, Hanuman Pujan, Bhairav Pujan, Kuldevi Pujan, Pitru Namaskar, Guru Vandana, Ganapathi Pujan, Maha Abhishek ( Ganapathi, Mahalakshmi, Hanuman, Kaal Bhairav ), Shodasopchar Pujan, Bhairav Mantra japa, Kaal Bhairav Ashtak Stotra paath, Kaal Bhairav Havan, Maha Lakshmi Havan, Dshansh Tarpan Maargan, Aarti, Pushpanjali, Kshama Prarthana, Maha Prasad, Ashirvad, Brahman Bhojan.

Puja Prasad For You

Energized Kaal Bhairav Yantra which should be kept at puja place, Rudraksha Japa Mala for chanting, Vibhuti should be applied by participants on the forehead, Mauli to be tied around the wrists of family members. Along with the Prasad, a Kaal Bhairav Booklet features powerful Mantras, stories, and teachings of Lord Kaal Bhairav.

About The Puja

Maha Mrityunjaya Anusthan is a special ritual where 7 Vedic Pandits recite the Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra, asking Lord Shiva for protection from illness, untimely death, and giving good health and strength.

Why Is This Puja For You?

  • It is a heartfelt prayer for a long, healthy life and for overcoming obstacles. This puja brings a deep sense of peace and strength, reminding us of our unity with nature and giving us inner resilience in difficult times.
  • This Anushthan involves the recitation of Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra along with Bilwarchanam and Havan for seeking the blessings of Lord Shiva.

Steps of the Puja

Shanti Paath, Devata Namaskar, Maha Sankalp, Ganga Pujan, Prithvi Pujan, Deepak Pujan, Surya Pujan, Dasho Disha Puajn, Hanuman Pujan, Bhairav Pujan, Kuldevi Pujan, Pitru Namaskar, Guru Vandana, Ganapathi Pujan, Maha Abhishek ( Ganpathi, Mahalakshmi, Hanuman, Sphatik Shivling ) , Maha Mrityunjaya Jap , Bilwarchan, Shodasopchar Pujan, Havan , Tarpan Marjan, Aarti, Pushpanjali, Kshama Prarthana, Maha Prasad, Ashirvad, Brahman Bhojan.
